Tixers

Tixers is changing the way tickets are exchanged

By trading your tickets to Tixers, we guarantee the value of your ticket and you receive points almost immediately that you can use for other tickets on Tixers. We will even send you suggestions on which games to attend.

Tixers will be launching soon, sign up to be notified when you can start trading your tickets.

    Sell

    Sell your tickets quickly

    1. Select your team and game then enter the location and price
    2. Forward us your tickets from your season ticket account
    3. Receive points to use for other tickets

    Buy

    Buying tickets on Tixers is easy

    1. Search for the location of the event
    2. Use existing points or purchase points to buy the tickets
    3. Print the tickets and enjoy the game
